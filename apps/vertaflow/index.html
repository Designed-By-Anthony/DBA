Questions we hear most.

When you sign up, you pick your industry vertical — Service Pro, Restaurant, Beauty, Retail, etc. VertaFlow automatically adapts its sidebar, terminology, pipeline stages, and feature modules to match how your business actually works. A roofer sees "Leads" and "Work Orders." A salon sees "Clients" and "Appointments." Same powerful platform underneath, but the experience feels purpose-built for you.

Yes. You can change your vertical at any time from Settings. Your data stays intact — only the UI labels, pipeline stage names, and visible modules change. You can even run a "General" vertical if none of the pre-built ones fit.

Absolutely. VertaFlow uses zero-trust, row-level tenant isolation on every single database query. Your data is cryptographically scoped to your organization — there is no "admin override" that can leak across tenants. This architecture is built to meet HIPAA and SOC 2 compliance standards.

Stripe. Invoices, subscriptions, POS terminal payments, and Connect payouts all run through Stripe. You get a real payment infrastructure — not a toy integration.
