VertaFlow FAQ

Core operational answers for every supported vertical configuration.

Can I switch verticals after signup?

Yes. You can switch between Service Pro, Retail, Restaurant, and other vertical views without losing tenant data.

Does VertaFlow work offline?

Yes. Leads and estimates queue locally while offline and sync automatically when the device is back online.

How does VertaFlow protect tenant data?

Tenant-scoped records remain isolated by org-level keys and row-level context in backend processing.
